What if a paper is smart enough to wake you up and act like an alarm?

What about a paper that can tell you current news or any important information?

Introducing
Smart Alarm

Who is it for?

Anyone who is busy in the morning.

  • Students who need to go to class.
  • Businessmen who want to figure out the traffic.
  • People who wants to know about the weather.
  • Anyone

How is this different from smart phone?

Sometimes, People don't come out a bed because they text and scroll through their endless newsfeed.

Smart Alarm only gives notifications of a day and does not give any extra newsfeed information from the past or future.

Also users are not able to type or text to their friends but only check the notification by viewing.

By only giving critical information for the day, it is more effective to users who are busy in the morning.

So, how does it work?

Your Alarm will go off

Slam the paper to snooze the alarm.

Pick up the paper to turn off the alarm.

Flip the paper to see the information inside.

Notification of the day will be listed on the screen.

Interfaces of the Alarm

There are three stage of the alarm.

Normal Setting: The paper remains white.

Alarmed Setting: The paper turns into a lighter shade of blue

Snooze Setting: The paper turns darker shade of blue.

Flip the page

Inside of the alarm,

Users can see the notifications of the day.

Breaking News will alert the user. User can click the article.

Click the box and see the article. User can exit the article by clicking the "x"
